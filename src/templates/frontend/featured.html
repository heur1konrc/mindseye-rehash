Featured Image

Sunset at Lake Monona

Camera Details

  • Camera: Canon EOS R8
  • Lens: RF 24-105mm f/4L IS USM
  • Aperture: f/8.0
  • Shutter Speed: 1/125
  • ISO: 100
  • Focal Length: 35mm

The Story Behind the Image

This image was captured during a perfect summer evening at Lake Monona in Madison. I had been waiting for weeks for the perfect conditions - clear skies with just enough clouds to catch the colors of sunset. As the sun began to dip below the horizon, the entire sky erupted in vibrant oranges and purples, reflecting perfectly in the calm waters of the lake. The silhouettes of the fishing piers created a perfect focal point against the colorful backdrop. This moment represents everything I love about photography in Wisconsin - the unexpected beauty that can appear in familiar places when the light is just right.
